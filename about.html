Fast-Travel

How it works

  1. Make a booking

    • Origin: You will need to type out a designated location of which our self-driving car can locate and pick you up. This is called the origin point, also called, point 'A', on the map.
    • Destination: Next, choose a destination, a location where our A.I. automated cars can take you. This is marked on the map as point 'B'. Please bear in mind. It will only deliver you from a location designated in the same country/region.
    • Map: Our map will then create the most efficient route for the car to travel along, from point 'A' to point 'B'.
    • Locate: Should you ever want to know your current location during your journey, there is a button on the map named 'Current Location'. This will place a marker on the exact location of where you are. * You will need to enable your location sharing services *
    • Time and Date: You can then set a time and date for your journey. You will see a quick summary of your booking before you continue to personalizing your experience.

  2. Personalize Your Trip

    • Correspondence: Add your name and email. This is how we and our vehicles will identify you as the client. Your email address will be used to receive correspondences from us regarding your booking.
    • Vehicle: You can specify the kind of vehicle you would like to be transported by or, in case you are not to bothered by this, we will allocate you a vehicle depending on the seating arrangement.
    • Music: Choose what theme of music you'd like to be played during your trip. You can always change this once you have entered the vehicle.
    • Payment Method: You can decide how you want to pay. The cost of the trip is dependent on the distance travelled and the vehicle used. Payment is always collected after the trip.
